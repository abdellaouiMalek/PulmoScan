Appointment FAQs

Common questions about appointments at PulmoScan

How do I prepare for my appointment?

Please bring any previous medical records, especially imaging studies, to your appointment. Arrive 15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

What if I need to reschedule my appointment?

You can reschedule your appointment by calling our office at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our office to verify that we accept your specific insurance.

How long will my appointment take?

Initial consultations typically last 45-60 minutes. Follow-up appointments are usually 30 minutes.
