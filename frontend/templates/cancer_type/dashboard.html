Understanding Lung Cancer Types

Lung cancer is classified into different types based on how the cells look under a microscope. Our AI model can help identify the type of lung cancer from SVS images.

Adenocarcinoma

Most common type of lung cancer, often found in the outer regions of the lung.

Squamous Cell

Often linked to smoking and typically found in the central part of the lungs.

Small Cell

Aggressive type that spreads quickly, strongly associated with smoking.

Normal Tissue

Healthy lung tissue with no signs of cancer cells.

About Our Model

ResNet-18 for Cancer Type Classification

Our model is a fine-tuned ResNet-18 architecture, specifically trained on SVS images to classify lung cancer types. The model analyzes the tissue patterns to make accurate predictions.

Key Features:
  • Trained on a large dataset of annotated SVS images
  • Achieves 92% accuracy in distinguishing between cancer types
  • Processes high-resolution SVS images with patch-based approach
  • Provides confidence scores for each prediction
  • Optimized for clinical use with fast inference time
