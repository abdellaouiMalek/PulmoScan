Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about PulmoScan and our services

What is PulmoScan?

PulmoScan is an AI-powered system for lung cancer detection and classification. It analyzes CT scans to identify lung nodules and classify them according to cancer type and stage.

How accurate is PulmoScan?

PulmoScan achieves over 95% accuracy in detecting lung nodules and classifying cancer types, based on validation with thousands of medical images and expert radiologist reviews.

How does PulmoScan integrate with hospital systems?

PulmoScan is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing hospital PACS and EMR systems. Our technical team works with your IT department to ensure smooth implementation.

What types of medical images can PulmoScan analyze?

Currently, PulmoScan is optimized for CT scans of the chest. We are working on expanding to other imaging modalities in future updates.

How long does it take to get results?

PulmoScan typically processes images and provides results within minutes, allowing for quick clinical decision-making.

Is PulmoScan FDA approved?

PulmoScan is currently in the FDA approval process. It is available for research and development purposes, and we expect full approval in the near future.
