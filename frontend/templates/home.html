    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions about PulmoScan and our services

    What is PulmoScan?

    PulmoScan is an AI-powered system for lung cancer detection and classification. It analyzes CT scans to identify lung nodules and classify them according to cancer type and stage.

    How accurate is PulmoScan?

    PulmoScan has achieved a 95% accuracy rate in clinical trials, significantly higher than conventional screening methods. The system continues to improve as it learns from more data.

    How does PulmoScan integrate with existing hospital systems?

    PulmoScan is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication System) and EHR (Electronic Health Record) systems. Our team provides full support for integration and customization.

    Is patient data secure with PulmoScan?

    Yes, PulmoScan adheres to strict data security protocols and is fully HIPAA compliant. All patient data is encrypted and stored securely.

    How can our hospital implement PulmoScan?

    Implementation is straightforward. Contact us to schedule a consultation, and our team will guide you through the process, from initial assessment to full deployment and training.
